The Fan Site about the International Seasons of Street Woman Fighter.

Street Woman Fighter

Street Woman Fighter is a Series that initally premiered back in August 18th 2021, and has had in total 3 seasons, and several spin off shows.

The two seasons I'll primarily be talking about are Season 3, World of Street Woman Fighter, and the spinoff series Street Woman Fighter Vietnam, also known as Nữ Hoàng Vũ Đạo Đường Phố.

Reason for Making this

What is it that makes me so compelled to talk about International Crews? Well, Street Woman Fighter is a predominantly Korean Show, due to both streaming on a Korean Network and also was filmed primarily in Korea, it's expected that a majority of the crews would be Koeran.

While, the Korean crews are amazing, I want to specifically talk about the international crews because they in general have a lot working against them, as a lot of the times there's language barriers, culture barriers, etc. Not to mention a lot of times they don't often get their flowers, so I'd love to have this oppertunity to do so.
